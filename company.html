About Us

Building the Future of E‑Commerce Advertising

We're an advertising technology startup on a mission to give every Shopify merchant the same advertising firepower that Fortune 500 companies take for granted.

The Problem We're Solving

Running a successful e-commerce store on Shopify is hard enough. But when it comes to advertising on Google, most small merchants face an impossible choice: hire an agency that charges $2,000–$10,000 per month, or try to figure out Google Ads on their own — a platform so complex that even experienced marketers struggle with it.

The result? Over 80% of Shopify merchants either don't advertise on Google at all, or waste their budget on poorly optimized campaigns. Meanwhile, their larger competitors dominate Google search results with professionally managed campaigns, capturing customers that should be theirs.

We saw this problem firsthand. As e-commerce operators ourselves, we experienced the frustration of trying to compete against bigger players with deeper pockets and dedicated marketing teams. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our Solution: AI as Your Ad Agency

Smart Ads AI was born from a simple idea: what if every Shopify merchant had their own AI-powered ad agency built right into their store?

Instead of paying thousands per month for a human agency that manages dozens of clients simultaneously, Smart Ads AI gives each merchant a dedicated AI engine that focuses exclusively on their store, their products, and their competitive landscape. The AI analyzes real Google search data, studies competitor strategies, and generates complete advertising campaigns — from keyword research to ad copy to budget recommendations.

The key difference from other tools: we don't just suggest keywords or write generic ad copy. Our AI acts like a senior campaign manager at a top ad agency — it analyzes your specific competitors, identifies gaps in their strategies, scores each product's advertising potential, and creates campaigns that are optimized for your unique market position.

Why We Built This

Smart Ads AI was founded in 2025 by an e-commerce entrepreneur who ran a Shopify store selling home textiles to US customers. After spending months trying to navigate Google Ads — and watching competitors outperform his store despite selling inferior products — he realized the playing field was fundamentally unfair.

Large brands had agencies and data teams optimizing their campaigns around the clock. Small merchants had YouTube tutorials and guesswork. The gap wasn't about product quality or customer service — it was about access to advertising intelligence.

That insight became the founding mission of Smart Ads AI: democratize Google Ads intelligence for every e-commerce merchant, regardless of budget or technical expertise.

What Makes Us Different

We're not another generic ad tool. We built Smart Ads AI around three core principles.

01

Real Data, Not Templates

Every recommendation our AI makes is based on real-time data from Google search results, competitor websites, and current market trends. We don't use generic templates or one-size-fits-all approaches. When our AI suggests keywords or writes ad copy for your products, it has already analyzed what your top 10+ competitors are doing, how much they're spending, and where the opportunities are.

02

Merchant Control First

We believe merchants should always be in the driver's seat. That's why every campaign we create starts in paused mode. No ad goes live, no budget is spent, and no changes are made to your Google Ads account without your explicit approval. You can review every keyword, edit every headline, and adjust every budget before anything goes live. This isn't just a feature — it's a fundamental design principle of our platform.

03

Built for Shopify, Not Bolted On

Smart Ads AI is a native Shopify embedded application — not a third-party tool with a Shopify integration added as an afterthought. The app lives inside your Shopify admin dashboard, syncs your products automatically via webhooks, and uses Shopify's billing system for subscriptions. You never leave your familiar Shopify environment.

Our Technology

We invest heavily in building a robust, secure, and scalable platform.

AI Intelligence Engine

At the core of Smart Ads AI is an intelligence engine powered by Anthropic's Claude AI — one of the world's most advanced large language models. Claude doesn't just generate text; it reasons about competitive dynamics, analyzes market positioning, and makes strategic recommendations. We feed it real data from Google search results, competitor websites, and your product catalog, and it produces actionable advertising strategies tailored to your specific situation.

Google Ads API Integration

We use the Google Ads API to create and manage campaigns directly in merchants' Google Ads accounts. This means campaigns created through our platform are real Google Ads campaigns — not simulations or previews. Merchants authenticate via OAuth 2.0, and we create campaigns with proper ad groups, keywords, responsive search ads, bidding strategies, and budget controls. We support both Search campaigns and Performance Max campaigns.

Competitive Intelligence Pipeline

Our competitor analysis system performs real-time Google searches for each product, scrapes competitor rankings and ad positions, analyzes keyword strategies, and estimates competitor ad spend. This data feeds directly into the AI engine, giving it the context it needs to make informed recommendations. We process data from multiple search API providers to ensure comprehensive coverage.

Security & Infrastructure

We take security seriously and have built our platform with enterprise-grade standards:

Authentication: OAuth 2.0 for Google Ads API access. Shopify session tokens for app authentication. No passwords are ever stored in our systems.

Data Encryption: PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase with encryption at rest. All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption. Redis caching via Upstash with encrypted connections.

Monitoring & Reliability: Sentry for real-time error monitoring and performance tracking. GitHub Actions for automated CI/CD pipeline. Rate limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse and ensure fair usage.

Compliance: Full compliance with Google Ads API Terms of Service, Google API Services User Data Policy, and Shopify App Store requirements. We never access customer PII, payment data, or order details from Shopify stores.
