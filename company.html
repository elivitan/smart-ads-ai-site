Our Mission

We believe every Shopify merchant deserves access to the same advertising intelligence that big brands and agencies use — without the big brand budget. Smart Ads AI automates the entire Google Ads workflow using artificial intelligence, from competitor research to campaign launch.

What We Build

Smart Ads AI is a Shopify embedded application that connects to a merchant's store, analyzes their products and competitive landscape, and creates optimized Google Ads campaigns. The platform uses Anthropic's Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, real-time search engine data for competitor analysis, and the Google Ads API for campaign management.

Technology & Security

Our platform is built with enterprise-grade security: OAuth 2.0 authentication for Google Ads, encrypted database on Supabase (PostgreSQL), HTTPS-only data transmission, and full compliance with Google's API Services User Data Policy. We never store Google passwords and merchants can disconnect at any time.
