Our Mission

We believe every Shopify merchant deserves access to the same advertising intelligence that big brands and agencies use — without the big brand budget. Traditional Google Ads management requires either expensive agency fees ($2,000–$10,000/month) or deep technical expertise that most small business owners don't have.

Smart Ads AI changes that equation. Our platform automates the entire Google Ads workflow using artificial intelligence — from competitive research to campaign launch — making professional-grade advertising accessible to any Shopify store owner.

What We Build

Smart Ads AI is a Shopify embedded application that connects to a merchant's store, analyzes their products and competitive landscape, and creates optimized Google Ads campaigns. The platform uses Anthropic's Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, real-time search engine data for competitor analysis, and the Google Ads API for direct campaign management.

Our app lives inside the Shopify admin dashboard — merchants never leave their familiar environment. Products sync automatically, competitors are analyzed in real time, and campaigns are created in paused mode so merchants always maintain full control.

Technology & Security

Our platform is built with enterprise-grade security and modern technology standards:

Authentication & Authorization: OAuth 2.0 for Google Ads API access. Shopify session tokens for app authentication. No passwords are ever stored.

Data Security: PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase with encryption at rest. All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption. Redis caching layer via Upstash with encrypted connections.

Compliance: Full compliance with Google Ads API Terms of Service, Google API Services User Data Policy, Shopify App Store requirements, and applicable data protection regulations.

Infrastructure: Sentry for real-time error monitoring and performance tracking. GitHub Actions for automated CI/CD pipeline. Rate limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse.

Data Handling: We access only product catalog data from Shopify (titles, descriptions, prices, images). We never access customer PII, payment information, or order details. Merchants can disconnect at any time, immediately revoking all data access.

Our Approach to Google Ads

Unlike tools that simply automate bidding or provide keyword suggestions, Smart Ads AI takes a holistic approach. We analyze your entire competitive landscape before generating a single ad. Every keyword recommendation, headline, and budget suggestion is backed by real data — not assumptions.

Crucially, we believe merchants should always be in control. That's why every campaign is created in paused mode. No ad goes live, no budget is spent, and no changes are made to your Google Ads account without your explicit approval. You can review, edit, or reject any recommendation.
