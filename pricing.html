Pricing FAQ

What's metered, and what isn't?

Only generative output is metered — AI image generations and AI video generations have a monthly quota per plan. Everything else runs continuously and is not counted: ad-copy generation, competitor scans, the 48 optimization cycles, organic-social publishing, comment and DM replies, decision log writes. Free includes 3 product scans. Starter: 30 images. Pro: 150 images + 10 videos. Premium: 500 images + 40 videos. Enterprise: unlimited.

Who has access to my ad accounts?

Only you. Bamzal connects to your ad accounts via each platform's official OAuth 2.0 flow with the minimum scopes required to create and manage campaigns on your behalf. All campaigns are created in your own Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X ad accounts. You can revoke access from inside the app at any time.

What if I want manual control?

The system has three autonomy tiers per platform, dial-able independently: shadow (the AI predicts but doesn't act), observe (the AI recommends, you approve), co-pilot (the AI executes within your caps). Run any platform fully manual, advisory, or autonomous — your call. Kill switch one click away.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. All paid plans (Starter, Pro, Premium, Enterprise) include a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.

Do I pay separately for ad spend?

Yes. Bamzal subscription covers the software platform. Your ad budgets on Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok and Pinterest are billed separately by each network directly to your own ad accounts on those platforms. We never handle your advertising spend.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from within the Shopify admin. Your campaigns remain in your Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X ad accounts — we never delete your work.
