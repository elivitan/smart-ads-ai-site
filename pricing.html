Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI credits?

AI credits are used each time Smart Ads AI performs a product scan, generates ad copy, or analyzes competitors. The Free plan includes 3 product scans. Starter includes 10 credits/month. Pro includes 200 credits/month.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Both Starter and Pro plans include a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.

Do I pay separately for ad spend?

Yes. Smart Ads AI subscription covers the software platform. Your ad budgets on Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok and Pinterest are billed separately by each network directly to your own ad accounts on those platforms. We never handle your advertising spend.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from within the Shopify admin. Your campaigns remain in your Google, Meta, TikTok and Pinterest ad accounts — we never delete your work.
