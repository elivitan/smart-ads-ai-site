Product Overview

An autonomous performance agency, embedded in your Shopify admin

Bamzal runs the disciplines of a full-service performance agency — strategy, media buying, creative, optimization, reporting, community management — as a coordinated team of 14 AI agents that work your accounts on Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X end-to-end, 24/7, with every decision logged and reversible.

What Is Bamzal?

Bamzal is an autonomous advertising agency that lives inside your Shopify admin. Where a traditional performance agency would staff your account with a strategist, a media buyer, a creative director, a community manager and a data analyst, Bamzal runs each of those disciplines as a specialist AI agent — coordinated through a single governance layer, never asleep, and accountable to an immutable decision log.

On install, Bamzal absorbs your catalog and your competitive landscape the way a senior strategist would on a kickoff: it scrapes Google search for the keywords your competitors are bidding on, models your category positioning, builds a profit-aware media plan, and drafts the first round of campaigns for every connected network.

From there it takes over the running of your accounts. It launches campaigns in paused mode for your first-time approval on Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X — and then handles daily bid adjustments, weekly portfolio rebalancing, creative DNA scoring and refresh cycles, monthly strategic re-planning, and in-the-moment replies to every comment and DM that hits your social channels. The AI does the work; you keep the keys, the data and the kill switch.

Your 14-Agent AI Team

Each role a performance agency would staff to your account exists inside Bamzal as a specialist AI agent. They coordinate through a single governance layer — every decision logged, every action reversible, every override yours.

The Strategist

Analyzes your catalog, margins and competitive landscape on install, then runs a monthly account-management cycle that re-plans against your goal, your inventory position and the previous month's results.

The Media Buyer

Owns daily bid management and weekly portfolio rebalancing across Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X. Shifts budget toward what's working, throttles what isn't, and never exceeds the caps you set.

The Creative Director

Generates ad copy, headlines, descriptions, and image briefs from your brand voice. Scores every creative with a DNA fitness model and rotates fresh variants when an existing one fatigues.

The Community Manager

Publishes scheduled posts on Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook, Threads and YouTube on your behalf — and replies to every comment, mention and DM in a human-sounding voice trained on your brand. Rate-budgeted, warmed-up gradually for new accounts, with an idempotency store that prevents double-posts and a pending-review queue for high-stakes messages.

The Analyst

Profit Intelligence engine computes net-profit ROAS (not gross) per campaign, factoring in your COGS, shipping and platform fees, so optimization targets actual margin instead of vanity revenue.

The Competitive Intel Lead

Continuously crawls Google for your category competitors, tracks their pricing, ad copy, keyword strategy and estimated spend, and surfaces gaps Bamzal can exploit.

The Inventory Coordinator

Listens to your Shopify inventory webhooks in real time. Out-of-stock SKUs get throttled or paused automatically so you stop paying for clicks that can't convert.

The Risk Officer

Search Sentinel detects waste keywords; Performance Insurance auto-pauses any campaign whose CPA breaches your threshold; HITL Sovereignty routes high-blast-radius decisions to a human approval queue.

The Decision Logger

Writes every bid change, budget shift, creative swap, pause, experiment outcome and reply to an immutable decision log. You can audit, override or roll back any action at any time.

The Experimenter

Designs Meta variant experiments, splits budget evenly, tracks winners and losers daily, then promotes the winner and demotes the loser — all through the same governance layer as every other mutation.

The Reporter

Transparent in-app narration explains every decision in plain language as it happens — "ran 200 simulations against the new competitor pricing", "rebalanced budget after detecting CTR decay on variant B". No black box.

The Supervisor

Brain-promotion engine evaluates 14-day correlation between predicted and realized ROAS per store. Stores that prove out get auto-promoted from shadow to observe to co-pilot autonomy tiers. Stores that drift get demoted.

What This Team Actually Ships

The day-to-day deliverables — what would show up on a weekly agency status report, except it happened automatically.

01

Automated Product Scanning

When you install Bamzal, it automatically pulls your product catalog from Shopify. The app analyzes each product's title, description, price, images, and category to understand your inventory. Products are synced via webhooks, so any changes in your Shopify store are reflected automatically.

02

Competitor Intelligence Engine

For each product, Bamzal searches Google to find your top competitors. The engine analyzes competitor websites, their Google rankings, ad copy, keyword strategies, and estimated ad spend. You see exactly who you're competing against — and where the gaps in their strategy are.

03

AI-Powered Ad Copy Generation

Using Anthropic's Claude AI, the platform generates complete ad copy packages: 15 headlines, 4 descriptions, keyword lists, and recommended bidding strategies. The AI bases its recommendations on real competitor data and current search trends — not generic templates.

04

Ad Score & Competitive Analysis

Each product receives an ad score (0–100) based on competitive landscape analysis. The score considers factors like keyword difficulty, competitor ad spend, search volume, and your product's positioning. Products with higher scores have better chances of profitable ad performance.

05

Multi-channel Ad Publishing (Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, X)

Bamzal uses the official APIs — Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API, TikTok Marketing API, Pinterest Ads API and X Ads API — to create campaigns directly in your own ad accounts on each network. Campaigns are always created in paused mode — you review the budget, audiences, keywords, creatives, ad copy and targeting before anything goes live. Supported: Google Search, Shopping and Performance Max; Meta Advantage+ Shopping, prospecting and retargeting; TikTok video and Spark Ads; Pinterest Shopping and standard campaigns; and X promoted posts and Takeover campaigns.

06

Budget Simulator

Before launching any campaign, use the built-in budget simulator to estimate clicks, impressions, and ROAS at different spend levels on each network. The simulator uses real data from Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X plus competitor benchmarks to give you realistic per-channel projections.

07

Real-Time Comment & DM Engagement (Pro plan and above)

Every two hours, Bamzal hunts your connected social channels for new comments, mentions and DMs on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and X — and replies in a human-sounding voice trained on your brand. The engagement engine runs a multi-module stack: Persona Engine + Humanization v2 + Voice Blender for tone; Brand-Primacy Guardian for brand consistency; Ethics Layer + Causal-Leakage detection to prevent off-brand or risky replies; Adaptive Rate Limiter + Anti-Detection Shield to avoid platform throttles and shadow bans; Conversation Memory + Narrative Continuity to keep thread context. Every reply is idempotency-checked, kill-switch protected, and a pending-review queue holds high-stakes messages for your approval.

08

Autonomous Social Publishing

Bamzal doesn't just run ads — it runs your organic social presence too. Scheduled posts go out to Instagram (feed, Stories, Reels), TikTok, X, Facebook, Threads and YouTube on a cadence the system tunes to each channel's audience peak windows. The Content Selector + Signal Hunter + Attention Economy modules decide what to post (trending topics, your new products, competitor moves, life moments and holidays). The Proactive Poster scheduler runs daily at 09:00 and 17:00 UTC for X by default and on-demand for everything else, with a Warmup Pipeline that ramps new accounts gradually so platforms don't flag them as automated.

09

AI Creative Studio

An in-app workspace where the Creative Director agent and you co-author every asset. AI copy generator drafts headlines, descriptions and captions per-platform (Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, X each have a dedicated copywriter module tuned to that platform's voice). Image generation runs on diffusion models; short-form video runs on Stability + ElevenLabs for voiceover. The Creative DNA library extracts the genetic attributes of your past best-performing ads (hooks, structures, CTAs, color palettes) and uses them to spawn fresh variants when an existing creative starts to fatigue. Assets connect to your Shopify product library, Google Drive and Dropbox — no manual asset wrangling.

10

24/7 Optimization & Safety Engines

Once campaigns are live, 48 scheduled cycles run the optimization loop: every 15 minutes a Google Ads micro-bid pass (±10% adjustments); every 2 hours a Search Sentinel waste-keyword sweep; every 3 hours a Meta optimizer; daily 08:00 UTC a Meta creative-refresh against fatigue scores; weekly Monday a portfolio rebalancer that shifts budget between campaigns under cap; Sunday a Self-Reflection cycle that synthesizes rules from the past week. Profit Intelligence forecasts net-profit ROAS (not gross) using your COGS and shipping. Inventory-Aware throttling auto-pauses out-of-stock SKUs in real time via Shopify webhooks. Performance Insurance auto-pauses any campaign whose CPA breaches your threshold. Every decision is capped, rate-limited, and written to an immutable log.

Built Like Enterprise Infrastructure

Bamzal isn't a "Claude wrapper" — it's a multi-agent system with operator-grade governance, audit trails, and SLOs. Everything the AI does is logged, capped, and reversible.

Governed Autonomy

Three autonomy tiers per store — shadow (AI predicts only), observe (AI recommends, human approves), co-pilot (AI executes within caps). Stores auto-promote between tiers only after the AI demonstrates a 14-day Pearson correlation ≥ 0.7 between predicted and realized ROAS. Operators can pin a store at any tier or pull the kill switch at any time.

Mutation Gateway

Every write to Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest or X is funnelled through a single gateway with per-platform adapters, per-shop isolation, a 12-step guard pipeline, and CI enforcement that rejects any code path that bypasses it. Nothing reaches an ad account without going through the gate.

Decision Log & SLOs

Every campaign launch, budget shift, creative refresh, and experiment outcome is timestamped and immutable. Seven binding SLOs (≥99% availability, ≥97% webhook delivery, <5 min queue lag, <15% gateway rejection, >95% job success) gate every deploy. If the system can't meet them, it pauses itself.

Multi-Agent Brain

14 specialist AI modules — decision engine, supervisor, cost guardrails, feature store, creative DNA scorer, causal-break detector, synthetic universe, account-manager cycle and others — plus 30+ domain engines (market crystallizer, opportunity detector, switching-cost moat, profit intelligence). Each agent has a defined role, audit surface, and override path.

Human-in-the-Loop Sovereignty

Low-confidence intents and high-blast-radius mutations (large budget shifts, pause-all, audience deletions) route to an approval queue before execution. You can dial the system from fully manual to fully autonomous per platform — independently for Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X.

Transparent Decision Surface

The in-app TransparencyLayer narrates what the AI is doing in plain language as it happens — "ran 200 simulations against the new competitor pricing", "self-mutation applied after detecting CTR decay on variant B". No black box.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — managed on behalf of clients
