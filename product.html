Supported Ad Channels

Bamzal publishes to the five largest paid channels for Shopify merchants — from a single campaign brief.

Google Ads

Search, Shopping and Performance Max. Keyword discovery, GAQL reporting, ConversionActionService for ROAS tracking.

Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

Advantage+ Shopping, retargeting and prospecting via the Meta Marketing API. Creative variations per placement; Pages + Instagram Business for organic-native assets.

TikTok Ads

Video, Spark Ads and catalog campaigns via the TikTok Marketing API. AI-generated hook + CTA variations tuned to TikTok's native feel.

Pinterest Ads

Shopping campaigns, standard and Idea Pins via the Pinterest Ads API. Product feed synced directly from Shopify, visual-first creative briefs.

X Ads

Promoted posts and Takeover campaigns via the X Ads API. AI-generated short-form copy tuned to the platform's conversational tone.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — managed on behalf of clients

Bamzal operates as a Tech Provider for Meta, creating and managing Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns on behalf of the Shopify merchants (business clients) who install our app. Merchants authorize access through Facebook Login for Business, granting our platform scoped permissions (ads_management, business_management, pages_show_list, instagram_basic) to publish campaigns into their own Meta Business & ad accounts. Merchants retain full ownership of their Meta assets and can revoke access at any time from Meta Business Integrations.

Technology Stack

Built with modern, enterprise-grade technology for reliability and security.

Shopify Integration

Embedded Shopify app using App Bridge. Installed directly from the Shopify App Store. Webhooks for real-time product sync.

AI Engine

Anthropic Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, competitive analysis, and strategic recommendations based on real data.

Ad Platform APIs

Direct integrations with Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API (Facebook, Instagram, Pages), TikTok Marketing API, Pinterest Ads API and X Ads API — for campaign creation, management, and performance reporting. Official OAuth 2.0 on every network for secure authentication.

Search Data

Real-time search engine data for competitor analysis, keyword research, and Google ranking tracking.

Database

PostgreSQL on Supabase with encrypted storage. All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption.

Infrastructure

Redis caching layer (Upstash), rate limiting, Sentry error monitoring, and GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline.

How Your Data Flows

Complete transparency on what data we access and how we use it.

Shopify Store

Product titles, descriptions, prices, and images are read from your Shopify store via secure API. We never access customer data or payment information.

→

AI Analysis

Your product data is sent to our AI engine along with search results data. The AI generates keywords, ad copy, and competitive scores.

→

Your Review

You review all generated content in the dashboard. Nothing is sent to Google, Meta, TikTok or Pinterest until you explicitly approve it.

→

Your Ad Accounts

Approved campaigns are created in paused mode across your Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads and Pinterest Ads accounts via each platform's official API. You control when they go live on every network.
