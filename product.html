Product Overview

The Complete Multi-Channel Ad Automation Platform for Shopify

Bamzal replaces the need for expensive ad agencies by combining competitor intelligence, AI copywriting, and direct API integrations with Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok Ads, Pinterest Ads and X Ads — all in one seamless Shopify app.

What Is Bamzal?

Bamzal is a Shopify embedded application that lives directly inside your Shopify admin dashboard. When you install it, the app automatically scans your product catalog and performs competitive intelligence analysis using real Google search data.

The platform uses Anthropic's Claude AI as its intelligence engine. Claude analyzes your competitors' ad strategies, identifies keyword opportunities, and generates optimized ad copy — acting like a senior ad agency campaign manager working exclusively on your store.

When you're ready to launch, Bamzal connects to your own Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X ad accounts via each platform's official OAuth 2.0 flow and creates campaigns in paused mode. You review everything before a single dollar is spent.

Core Capabilities

Everything you need to create high-performing ad campaigns across every major network — without being a paid-ads expert.

01

Automated Product Scanning

When you install Bamzal, it automatically pulls your product catalog from Shopify. The app analyzes each product's title, description, price, images, and category to understand your inventory. Products are synced via webhooks, so any changes in your Shopify store are reflected automatically.

02

Competitor Intelligence Engine

For each product, Bamzal searches Google to find your top competitors. The engine analyzes competitor websites, their Google rankings, ad copy, keyword strategies, and estimated ad spend. You see exactly who you're competing against — and where the gaps in their strategy are.

03

AI-Powered Ad Copy Generation

Using Anthropic's Claude AI, the platform generates complete ad copy packages: 15 headlines, 4 descriptions, keyword lists, and recommended bidding strategies. The AI bases its recommendations on real competitor data and current search trends — not generic templates.

04

Ad Score & Competitive Analysis

Each product receives an ad score (0–100) based on competitive landscape analysis. The score considers factors like keyword difficulty, competitor ad spend, search volume, and your product's positioning. Products with higher scores have better chances of profitable ad performance.

05

Multi-channel Ad Publishing (Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, X)

Bamzal uses the official APIs — Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API, TikTok Marketing API, Pinterest Ads API and X Ads API — to create campaigns directly in your own ad accounts on each network. Campaigns are always created in paused mode — you review the budget, audiences, keywords, creatives, ad copy and targeting before anything goes live. Supported: Google Search, Shopping and Performance Max; Meta Advantage+ Shopping, prospecting and retargeting; TikTok video and Spark Ads; Pinterest Shopping and standard campaigns; and X promoted posts and Takeover campaigns.

06

Budget Simulator

Before launching any campaign, use the built-in budget simulator to estimate clicks, impressions, and ROAS at different spend levels on each network. The simulator uses real data from Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X plus competitor benchmarks to give you realistic per-channel projections.

07

AI Chat Replies — Comments & DMs (Pro plan and above)

Bamzal can also reply to comments and DMs on your behalf across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and X — in a human-sounding voice trained on your brand. Every reply is rate-budgeted, audit-logged and kill-switch protected, with a pending-review queue for high-stakes messages. Available on Pro, Premium and Enterprise plans.

08

24/7 Optimization & Safety Engines

Once a campaign is live, the system runs continuous optimization: Profit Intelligence (net-profit ROAS forecasting), Inventory-Aware throttling (so out-of-stock products stop spending), Search Sentinel (waste-keyword detection), Performance Insurance (auto-pause when CPA exceeds your target), and a creative-evolution loop that retires fatigued ads. All budget/bid changes are capped and audited.

Supported Ad Channels

Bamzal publishes to the five largest paid channels for Shopify merchants — from a single campaign brief.

Google Ads

Search, Shopping and Performance Max. Keyword discovery, GAQL reporting, ConversionActionService for ROAS tracking.

Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

Advantage+ Shopping, retargeting and prospecting via the Meta Marketing API. Creative variations per placement; Pages + Instagram Business for organic-native assets.

TikTok Ads

Video, Spark Ads and catalog campaigns via the TikTok Marketing API. AI-generated hook + CTA variations tuned to TikTok's native feel.

Pinterest Ads

Shopping campaigns, standard and Idea Pins via the Pinterest Ads API. Product feed synced directly from Shopify, visual-first creative briefs.

X Ads

Promoted posts and Takeover campaigns via the X Ads API. AI-generated short-form copy tuned to the platform's conversational tone.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — managed on behalf of clients

Bamzal operates as a Tech Provider for Meta, creating and managing Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns on behalf of the Shopify merchants (business clients) who install our app. Merchants authorize access through Facebook Login for Business, granting our platform scoped permissions (ads_management, business_management, pages_show_list, instagram_basic) to publish campaigns into their own Meta Business & ad accounts. Merchants retain full ownership of their Meta assets and can revoke access at any time from Meta Business Integrations.

Technology Stack

Built with modern, enterprise-grade technology for reliability and security.

Shopify Integration

Embedded Shopify app using App Bridge. Installed directly from the Shopify App Store. Webhooks for real-time product sync.

AI Engine

Anthropic Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, competitive analysis, and strategic recommendations based on real data.

Ad Platform APIs

Direct integrations with Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API (Facebook, Instagram, Pages), TikTok Marketing API, Pinterest Ads API and X Ads API — for campaign creation, management, and performance reporting. Official OAuth 2.0 on every network for secure authentication.

Search Data

Real-time search engine data for competitor analysis, keyword research, and Google ranking tracking.

Database

PostgreSQL on Supabase with encrypted storage. All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption.

Infrastructure

Redis caching layer (Upstash), rate limiting, Sentry error monitoring, and GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline.

How Your Data Flows

Complete transparency on what data we access and how we use it.

Shopify Store

Product titles, descriptions, prices, and images are read from your Shopify store via secure API. We never access customer data or payment information.

→

AI Analysis

Your product data is sent to our AI engine along with search results data. The AI generates keywords, ad copy, and competitive scores.

→

Your Review

You review all generated content in the dashboard. Nothing is sent to Google, Meta, TikTok or Pinterest until you explicitly approve it.

→

Your Ad Accounts

Approved campaigns are created in paused mode across your Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads and Pinterest Ads accounts via each platform's official API. You control when they go live on every network.
