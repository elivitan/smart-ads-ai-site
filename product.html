Product Overview

The Complete Multi-Channel Ad Automation Platform for Shopify

Smart Ads AI replaces the need for expensive ad agencies by combining competitor intelligence, AI copywriting, and direct API integrations with Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok Ads and Pinterest Ads — all in one seamless Shopify app.

What Is Smart Ads AI?

Smart Ads AI is a Shopify embedded application that lives directly inside your Shopify admin dashboard. When you install it, the app automatically scans your product catalog and performs competitive intelligence analysis using real Google search data.

The platform uses Anthropic's Claude AI as its intelligence engine. Claude analyzes your competitors' ad strategies, identifies keyword opportunities, and generates optimized ad copy — acting like a senior ad agency campaign manager working exclusively on your store.

When you're ready to launch, Smart Ads AI connects to your own Google, Meta, TikTok and Pinterest ad accounts via each platform's official OAuth 2.0 flow and creates campaigns in paused mode. You review everything before a single dollar is spent.

Core Capabilities

Everything you need to create high-performing ad campaigns across every major network — without being a paid-ads expert.

01

Automated Product Scanning

When you install Smart Ads AI, it automatically pulls your product catalog from Shopify. The app analyzes each product's title, description, price, images, and category to understand your inventory. Products are synced via webhooks, so any changes in your Shopify store are reflected automatically.

02

Competitor Intelligence Engine

For each product, Smart Ads AI searches Google to find your top competitors. The engine analyzes competitor websites, their Google rankings, ad copy, keyword strategies, and estimated ad spend. You see exactly who you're competing against — and where the gaps in their strategy are.

03

AI-Powered Ad Copy Generation

Using Anthropic's Claude AI, the platform generates complete ad copy packages: 15 headlines, 4 descriptions, keyword lists, and recommended bidding strategies. The AI bases its recommendations on real competitor data and current search trends — not generic templates.

04

Ad Score & Competitive Analysis

Each product receives an ad score (0–100) based on competitive landscape analysis. The score considers factors like keyword difficulty, competitor ad spend, search volume, and your product's positioning. Products with higher scores have better chances of profitable ad performance.

05

Multi-channel Ad Publishing (Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest)

Smart Ads AI uses the official APIs — Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API, TikTok Marketing API and Pinterest Ads API — to create campaigns directly in your own ad accounts on each network. Campaigns are always created in paused mode — you review the budget, audiences, keywords, creatives, ad copy and targeting before anything goes live. Supported: Google Search, Shopping and Performance Max; Meta Advantage+ Shopping, prospecting and retargeting; TikTok video and Spark Ads; and Pinterest Shopping and standard campaigns.

06

Budget Simulator

Before launching any campaign, use the built-in budget simulator to estimate clicks, impressions, and ROAS at different spend levels on each network. The simulator uses real data from Google, Meta, TikTok and Pinterest plus competitor benchmarks to give you realistic per-channel projections.

Supported Ad Channels

Smart Ads AI publishes to the four largest paid channels for Shopify merchants — from a single campaign brief.

🔎 Google Ads

Search, Shopping and Performance Max. Keyword discovery, GAQL reporting, ConversionActionService for ROAS tracking.

📘 Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

Advantage+ Shopping, retargeting and prospecting via the Meta Marketing API. Creative variations per placement; Pages + Instagram Business for organic-native assets.

🎵 TikTok Ads

Video, Spark Ads and catalog campaigns via the TikTok Marketing API. AI-generated hook + CTA variations tuned to TikTok's native feel.

📌 Pinterest Ads

Shopping campaigns, standard and Idea Pins via the Pinterest Ads API. Product feed synced directly from Shopify, visual-first creative briefs.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — managed on behalf of clients

Smart Ads AI operates as a Tech Provider for Meta, creating and managing Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns on behalf of the Shopify merchants (business clients) who install our app. Merchants authorize access through Facebook Login for Business, granting our platform scoped permissions (ads_management, business_management, pages_show_list, instagram_basic) to publish campaigns into their own Meta Business & ad accounts. Merchants retain full ownership of their Meta assets and can revoke access at any time from Meta Business Integrations.

Technology Stack

Built with modern, enterprise-grade technology for reliability and security.

Shopify Integration

Embedded Shopify app using App Bridge. Installed directly from the Shopify App Store. Webhooks for real-time product sync.

AI Engine

Anthropic Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, competitive analysis, and strategic recommendations based on real data.

Ad Platform APIs

Direct integrations with Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API (Facebook, Instagram, Pages), TikTok Marketing API and Pinterest Ads API — for campaign creation, management, and performance reporting. Official OAuth 2.0 on every network for secure authentication.

Search Data

Real-time search engine data for competitor analysis, keyword research, and Google ranking tracking.

Database

PostgreSQL on Supabase with encrypted storage. All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption.

Infrastructure

Redis caching layer (Upstash), rate limiting, Sentry error monitoring, and GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline.
