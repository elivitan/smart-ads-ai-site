Autonomous Growth System for Shopify

One system. The entire growth side of your store.

Paid media across Google, Meta, TikTok, Pinterest and X. Organic social across Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook, Threads and YouTube. What Bamzal is

Bamzal is a single system that takes over the commercial surface of your Shopify store — the part where revenue is generated and defended. It plans the strategy, buys the media, writes the creative, publishes the content, replies to the conversations, watches the competitors, forecasts the demand, balances the budget, refreshes the ads, and reports the outcomes. It does all of this on its own — but every decision flows through a governance layer that you control, a decision log that you can audit, and a kill switch that's one click away.

It's not a media-buying tool, a social-management tool or a creative tool. It's the system those tools become when they're consolidated, given memory, allowed to coordinate, and trusted to act.

What the system covers

Seven surfaces. One coordinated system.

Acquisition

Paid campaigns across Google Search, Shopping, Performance Max, YouTube, Meta Advantage+, TikTok Spark, Pinterest Shopping and X promoted posts. Created in your own ad accounts, in paused mode, through a unified Mutation Gateway.

Presence

Organic content scheduled and published to Instagram (feed, Stories, Reels), TikTok, X, Facebook, Threads and YouTube. Cadence tuned per channel. Warmup pipeline for new accounts so platforms don't flag automation.

Creative

An in-app studio for copy, image and short-form video. Per-platform copywriter modules tuned to each network's voice. Diffusion-based image generation. Stability + ElevenLabs video. A DNA library that learns what's working and spawns variants when ads fatigue.

Community

Real-time replies to every comment, mention and DM on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and X. A multi-module engagement stack — Persona Engine, Humanization, Voice Blender, Brand-Primacy Guardian, Ethics Layer, Adaptive Rate Limiter, Conversation Memory.

Intelligence

Continuous Google crawling of your category competitors — pricing, ad copy, keyword strategy, estimated spend. Profit Intelligence that forecasts net-profit ROAS using your COGS. Causal-break detection. Cross-store benchmarks via differential-privacy hive sync.

Optimization

Forty-eight scheduled cycles — 15-minute micro-bid passes, 2-hour Search Sentinel waste sweeps, 3-hour Meta optimizer, daily creative refresh, weekly portfolio rebalancer, Sunday self-reflection. Inventory-Aware throttling auto-pauses out-of-stock SKUs in real time.

Governance

Twelve-step Mutation Gateway in front of every platform write. Immutable Decision Log on every action. Seven binding production SLOs that pause the system if breached. Three autonomy tiers per platform, independently dial-able. Human-in-the-Loop approval queue for high-blast-radius actions.

Self-Learning

The Self-Reflection cycle synthesizes rules from the prior week's outcomes. The Evolution Orchestrator runs genetic-algorithm tuning of bidding policies. The Brain Promotion engine evaluates 14-day correlation between predicted and realized ROAS and auto-promotes individual AI agents from shadow to observe to co-pilot tiers.

Compliance

Shopify GDPR webhooks (customer redact, shop redact, data request) wired. Meta GDPR data-deletion callback. EU AI Act Article 11 transparency surfaces. CSP nonce-based, OAuth 2.0 with PKCE where supported. TLS 1.2+ everywhere.

Supported Ad Channels

Bamzal publishes to the five largest paid channels for Shopify merchants — from a single campaign brief.

Google Ads

Search, Shopping and Performance Max. Keyword discovery, GAQL reporting, ConversionActionService for ROAS tracking.

Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

Advantage+ Shopping, retargeting and prospecting via the Meta Marketing API. Creative variations per placement; Pages + Instagram Business for organic-native assets.

TikTok Ads

Video, Spark Ads and catalog campaigns via the TikTok Marketing API. AI-generated hook + CTA variations tuned to TikTok's native feel.

Pinterest Ads

Shopping campaigns, standard and Idea Pins via the Pinterest Ads API. Product feed synced directly from Shopify, visual-first creative briefs.

X Ads

Promoted posts and Takeover campaigns via the X Ads API. AI-generated short-form copy tuned to the platform's conversational tone.

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — managed on behalf of clients

Bamzal operates as a Tech Provider for Meta, creating and managing Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns on behalf of the Shopify merchants (business clients) who install our app. Merchants authorize access through Facebook Login for Business, granting our platform scoped permissions (ads_management, business_management, pages_show_list, instagram_basic) to publish campaigns into their own Meta Business & ad accounts. Merchants retain full ownership of their Meta assets and can revoke access at any time from Meta Business Integrations.
