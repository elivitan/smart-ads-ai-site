About Smart Ads AI

The smarter way to advertise on Google.

The Problem

Most Shopify merchants know they should advertise on Google, but Google Ads is complex, expensive, and time-consuming. Hiring an agency costs hundreds or thousands per month.

Our Solution

Smart Ads AI is a Shopify app that automates the entire Google Ads workflow. Our AI scans your products, researches your competitors, generates optimized ad copy, and creates ready-to-launch campaigns in under 60 seconds.

We believe every Shopify merchant deserves access to the same advertising intelligence that big brands and agencies use, without the big brand budget.

How It Works

When you install Smart Ads AI, the app syncs your Shopify product catalog and uses AI to analyze each product. We search Google for your competitors, analyze their ad strategies and keywords, and use that intelligence to generate optimized campaigns.

The app connects to your Google Ads account via secure OAuth 2.0 authentication. We create campaigns on your behalf, but you always stay in control — all campaigns launch paused so you can review them before spending.

Technology

Smart Ads AI integrates with the Google Ads API for campaign management, Anthropic's Claude AI for intelligent ad copy generation, and SerpAPI/Serper for real-time search engine data.

Application Status

Smart Ads AI is currently in active development. The application is being built as a Shopify embedded app and will be published to the Shopify App Store upon completion of development and testing. We are applying for Google Ads API access to enable campaign creation and management features.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Smart Ads AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Smart Ads AI application (the "Service"), a Shopify app that helps merchants create and manage Google Ads campaigns. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Shopify Store Data: Product titles, descriptions, prices, images, and inventory status. Used solely to generate AI-powered ad campaigns.

Google Ads Data: Campaign performance data including impressions, clicks, cost, conversions, and campaign settings. Accessed via OAuth 2.0 with your explicit consent.

Account Information: Shopify store URL, shop name, and email address as provided by Shopify during installation.

Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including features used and scan history.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Generate AI-powered ad copy and campaign recommendations
  • Create and manage Google Ads campaigns on your behalf
  • Display campaign performance metrics in your dashboard
  • Analyze competitor data to improve your advertising strategy
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Google Ads API Data

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • We only access Google Ads data necessary for our Service
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your Google Ads data with third parties
  • We do not use Google Ads data for advertising purposes unrelated to our Service
  • We do not use Google Ads data for training AI models beyond providing the Service to you
  • Access is limited to employees and systems that need it to operate the Service

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Anthropic (Claude AI) for ad copy generation and SerpAPI for search data. These do not retain your data.
  • Google: Campaign data sent to Google Ads API to manage campaigns on your behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or valid legal process.

6. Data Security

  • All data transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • OAuth 2.0 for Google Ads authentication — we never store your Google password
  • Database encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits

7. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. When you uninstall the app, we delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
