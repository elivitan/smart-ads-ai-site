Frequently Asked Questions

Will the app spend money without my approval?

Absolutely not. All campaigns are created in paused mode inside your own Google Ads account. You must explicitly approve and enable each campaign before any ad goes live or any budget is spent.

Do I need Google Ads experience?

No. Smart Ads AI handles everything — keyword research, ad copy writing, competitor analysis, and campaign structure. You just review and approve. The AI acts as your personal ad agency.

How does the AI generate ad copy?

We use Anthropic's Claude AI, which analyzes your product data, competitor strategies, and real search trends to generate relevant, high-quality ad copy. Each headline and description is scored based on predicted performance.

What data do you access from my store?

We read your product titles, descriptions, prices, and images from Shopify to perform competitive analysis. We never access customer data, payment information, or order details.

Can I disconnect at any time?

Yes. Uninstalling the app or disconnecting your Google Ads account immediately revokes all access. Your campaigns remain in your Google Ads account — we never delete your work.
